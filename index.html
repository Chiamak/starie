Meet The CEO

I'm Amaka, owner of starieluxuryhairs, mother of great kids and a shameless explorer of new experience, for some years now i have felt like a fashionista stuck in the wig production industry- no joke i'm a qualified wig trader for over 6 years now, i am also a qualified and certified personal trainer while i have spent quite a bit of time searching for my passion, i've found that following it is where the real fun and challenge begins. I am inspired by my love of music and travel, i acted instinctually by choosing this new journey into the world of fashion with pride.
